Science Bowl Summer Tournament

The (unofficial) National Science Bowl Discord server is proud to announce the online Science Bowl Summer Tournament! Students will be given the opportunity to compete with teams across the country in a fast-paced buzzer competition in math and science. The competition will consist of a Round Robin and Double Elimination format, with the top 16 teams advancing to the afternoon tournament. Depending on interest, there may be divisions separated by skill, and teams that do not qualify for the afternoon tournament may be allowed to play friendly matches. The finals will be broadcast live. The early registration deadline is July 14th, the standard registration deadline is July 26th, and the tournament is held on August 7th. The competition will follow official NSB rules for scoring, timekeeping, etc. modified for an online format. If you have any questions regarding the tournament, check out the FAQ section. Feel free to ask in the SBST Discord or directly to the staff through email for any unresolved questions.
